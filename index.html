Feeling a product, digitally

Traditional media files - image, video, sound - are limited to Two dimensional experience. 3D object files add a new level of user engagement.  

Depth

Users can zoom in and out and turn the object around to see the entire physical object on all it sides and in its realistic dimensions and ratio

Angle Turning

Users can turn the object around 360° degrees, as well as up and down, to get a complete view

Immerse in the physical environment of the user

Using mobile devices, users can 'position' the digital object on the camera screen in their physical world

How it works on your website

Any product image that has a 3D object attached features a '3D button' watermark at the top right hand side.
When the user presses on the image the 3D model shows up and the user can interact with it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Browsers & Devices

The 3D models are now supported on all major browsers and on all platforms (desktop and mobile). In Apple devices running iOS 12 the models are opened seamlessly using the iOS "AR quick view". On all other platforms (desktop and Android) a viewer is used to show the models. 

Integrations

Our models and viewer work on all major e-commerce platforms including Shopify, Magento, Big commerce and Woo commerce. There is a short HTML code snippet to ensure 3D models support.   

Implementation

We can add the models to your website for a small fee or you can do it yourself using our guide (which we send you together with the models). 
Adding the models requires just a small addition of HTML code snippet.  

Pricing

Models prices vary between $70-$150 depending on the physical shape of the product. Complex products with a relatively high number of angles and curves will be slightly more expensive as they are more complex to produce. We provide an accurate quote within 48 hours.     

Is there a bulk-buy discount?

Yes there is! Bulk buys are typically defined as orders of 30 and more models and in these cases we can provide a more cost efficient proposal for the entire package of objects.
